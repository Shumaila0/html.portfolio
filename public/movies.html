Top Anime movies/ series according to me

My top list

Your lie in April

Its a story about A piano prodigy who lost his ability to play after suffering a traumatic event in his childhood is forced back into the spotlight by an eccentric girl with a secret of her own.

One piece

ONE PIECE is a legendary high-seas quest unlike any other. It shows how someone chooses to go after their dreams will ultimately define them no matter what the circumstances are.

Silent voice

A Silent Voice truly touches on the core idea of how we choose to treat others and teaches us some important life lessons, such as embracing others despite awkwardness and fear, apologising and seeking redemption for past wrongs and to treat each other with respect and kindness.
